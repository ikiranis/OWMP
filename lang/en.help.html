Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Space: Play/Pause
  • N: Next Song
  • P: Previous Song
  • F: Enter/Exit fullscreen
  • I: Display/Hide overlay overlay
  • G: Activate/deactivate Giphy on mp3s
  • ↑/↓: Up/Down the volume
  • M: Mute
  • ←/→: Forward/Backward 1 minute on the song
  • ‹/› or 7/8: Forward/Backward playback speed
  • ? or 9: Normal playback speed
  • 1-5: Rating song
  • L: Toggle Live/Official for the song
  • A: Add song to active manual playlist
  • D: Remove song from active manual playlist
  • E: Change the chozen manual playlist
  • Q: Add song to queue
  • O: Enable/disable overlay on fullscreen
